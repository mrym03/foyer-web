[ The Product ]

One embed. A full sales motion.

01

Speaks for your brand

Foyer crawls your entire site and builds a private knowledge base. The voice agent answers every visitor question using only your content — no hallucinations, no off-topic tangents, no guessing. It knows your pricing, your features, your differentiators. It sounds like your best salesperson.

02

Navigates in real time

This is the part nobody else does. When a visitor asks about pricing, Foyer scrolls the page there as it explains. Ask about a feature — it highlights it. Ask to see an example — it takes you there. The page moves as the conversation does. Visitors don't get lost. They get guided.

03

Captures and converts

Foyer reads buying intent in real time. When a visitor is ready, it asks for their name and email naturally — inside the conversation. It books a call with your founders directly. No redirect. No separate scheduling tool. The conversion happens in the voice. Every lead, every transcript, lands in your dashboard.

[ How It Works ]

Live on your site in three steps.

01

Paste one script tag

Drop a single line of code onto your site. No npm. No install. No framework requirements. Works on Webflow, WordPress, Framer, React — anything.

<script src="https://foyer.ink/embed.js"
        data-site="your-domain.com"></script>
02

We index your site

Foyer crawls every page — features, pricing, FAQs, case studies, all of it — and builds a private knowledge base in about two minutes. Your agent is trained on your content only. Nothing is shared. Nothing is generic.

03

Your agent goes live

A floating voice button appears on your site. Visitors click it, speak naturally, and your AI salesperson handles the rest — answering, navigating, capturing, converting. You watch it all in your dashboard in real time.
