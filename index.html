Your website is a brochure.

Right now, a visitor lands on your site. They scroll. They try to understand what you do. They have questions about pricing, fit, and whether to sign up. They do not ask. They leave. You never find out why.

A contact form won't save them. A chatbot feels like a wall. A salesperson can't be there at midnight.

[ The Product ]

One embed. A full sales motion.

01

Speaks for your brand

Foyer crawls your entire site and builds a private knowledge base. The voice agent answers every visitor question using only your content, with no hallucinations, no off-topic tangents, and no guessing. It knows your pricing, your features, and your differentiators. It sounds like your best salesperson.

02

Navigates in real time

This is the part nobody else does. When a visitor asks about pricing, Foyer scrolls the page there as it explains. Ask about a feature, it highlights it. Ask to see an example, it takes you there. The page moves as the conversation does. Visitors do not get lost. They get guided.

03

Captures and converts

Foyer reads buying intent in real time. When a visitor is ready, it asks for their name and email naturally inside the conversation. The conversion happens in the voice, and every lead and transcript lands in your dashboard with context attached.

[ How It Works ]

Live on your site in three steps.

01

Paste your embed snippet

Create an agent in the app, copy the install snippet, and drop it onto your site. No npm. No framework lock-in. Works on Webflow, WordPress, Shopify, Framer, React, and plain HTML.

<script src="https://app.foyer.ink/static/widget.js"
        data-agent-id="your-agent-id"
        data-server="https://app.foyer.ink" async></script>
02

We index your site

Foyer crawls every page, including features, pricing, FAQs, and case studies, then builds a private knowledge base in about two minutes. Your agent is trained on your content only. Nothing is shared. Nothing is generic.

03

Your agent goes live

A floating voice button appears on your site. Visitors click it, speak naturally, and your AI salesperson handles the rest: answering, navigating, capturing, and converting. You watch it all in your dashboard in real time.
